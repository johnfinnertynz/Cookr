# App Store Readiness

## App Metadata

Name:
Cookr

Subtitle:
Easy NZ dinners without takeaway

Short description:
Low-effort recipes, guided cooking, and NZ grocery lists for nights when takeaway feels too easy.

Long description:
Cookr helps New Zealanders cook realistic dinners with less stress. Choose your energy level, time, budget, goals, appliances, and pantry items. Cookr recommends beginner-friendly meals, builds a practical grocery list, links ingredients to Woolworths NZ searches where possible, and guides you one step at a time while you cook.

Primary keywords:
meal planner, NZ recipes, grocery list, beginner cooking, high protein, cheap dinners, fakeaway, Woolworths, meal prep, easy dinner

## Feature Descriptions

- Tonight modes: No energy, Cook in 15, Post-gym, Use pantry, Normal night.
- Beginner-safe cooking mode with visual cues, timers, panic help, and substitutions.
- Grocery list grouped by aisle/category with pantry staples separated.
- Woolworths NZ search assistant with estimated pricing.
- Saved rotations and gentle repeat prompts.
- Low-waste planning with ingredient overlap and leftovers.

## Privacy Policy Draft

Cookr collects the information needed to recommend meals and improve the product, including cooking preferences, dietary preferences, pantry items, saved recipes, cooking feedback, and app usage events. Cookr does not sell personal information. Health, diet, weight, and fitness preferences are treated as sensitive preference data and should be collected only with clear user consent.

Users should be able to:
- Access their profile data.
- Delete their account and associated profile data.
- Opt out of non-essential analytics.
- Request export of saved recipes, pantry, and meal plan data.

Analytics should avoid storing unnecessary raw free text. Operational logs should not include private pantry, diet, or health details unless required for debugging and explicitly protected.

## Terms Draft

Cookr provides recipe, shopping, nutrition, and product matching assistance for convenience only. Cooking times, prices, nutrition, availability, and product matches are estimates and may vary. Users are responsible for checking allergens, dietary suitability, food safety, retailer prices, and product availability.

Cookr does not provide medical advice. Nutrition and weight-related features are informational only.

Woolworths NZ links are user-controlled search links. Cookr does not represent that it is affiliated with Woolworths NZ unless a formal partnership exists.

Recipe content must be original, licensed, public-domain, user-provided with rights, or otherwise legally permitted.

## App Icon Concepts
